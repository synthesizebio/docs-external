---

Use these steps to connect the production Synthesize Bio integration in Claude.

## 1. Open Claude integrations

In Claude, open **Settings** and go to **Integrations**.

If the label in your Claude surface is slightly different, look for the area where you add a new integration or connector.

## 2. Add the connector URL

Paste the server URL below:

```text
https://app.synthesize.bio/api/mcp
```

## 3. Sign in to Synthesize Bio

Claude will prompt you to authenticate with your Synthesize Bio account. Complete the sign-in flow in your browser when prompted.

You do not need a separate account just for the connector. Your normal Synthesize Bio account is used for authentication.

## 4. Confirm the integration is available

After the connection succeeds, Claude should be able to use the Synthesize Bio analysis tools in a conversation.

## First-use checklist

- The connector URL was entered exactly as shown above.
- You completed the browser sign-in flow.
- Claude shows the integration as connected.
