Synthesize Bio builds foundation models for biology. Our platform lets you query predictive models, run analyses, and integrate biological intelligence into your tools and workflows.

These docs cover the platform itself, the Python and R SDKs, and the MCP integration for connecting Synthesize Bio to AI assistants like Claude and Cursor.
